Our Preamble

Our Motivation: The ineffable Connect!

This child mother analogy is what the feel is all about...

Just like a mother carries and nurtures her unborn child for a span of 9 months like so a farmer cultivates, looks after and nurtures his crops for a span of around 10 months, (this figure may vary from crop to crop) in the very same manner... Just imagine a situation that entire of the almost yearlong efforts of a farmer go in vain; this would simply be nothing but the death of the child like crops of that farmer, the death of his yearlong efforts, the financial support which these assets of him were supposed to give him would be halted, this would eventually lead to the death of that very farmer... This was from a single farmer's point of view, this would be the case of hundreds of various farmers working parallely accross those fields day and night! To prevent such situations and giving this "Ineffable Connect" the top most priority apart from other impacts of wasting of crops has led us to devise this web service.



Effects

  • There arises great problem while dumping such crops.
  • The economy declines because the overall import and export gets paralyzed
  • The daily life fuel gets null and void of consumption thereby ill effecting growth and development
  • The families of the farmer earn their daily breads by providing us our daily breads, which in such case they fail to and eventually fail theirs as well!

Vision and Objectives

This is a portal to target all of the above mentioned issues either directly or indirectly and in a crux, it is aimed to stop the crops that are stocked out there in the warehouses from wasting....

The working model achieves it's functionalities as follows:

The farmer or the warehouse keeper/owner who has been victimized by this problem registers him on the portal by filling in all the relevant details. Another farmer or the warehouse keeper or any such person who has such stocks even at the local level registers himself. Eventually a large amount of data gets accumulated at the backend on the databases. This database has all the relevant of the location of warehouses, stock locations, etc. This database also has the amount and the types of crops being stored along with the estimated date by which the particular crop/grain in question would get spoiled and this date is reliable enough as it is being fed by the person who monitors the stock All the data of this database then gets forwarded to all of the concerned and competent authorities on the local and the national level, after sorting of the data and this way the authorised personnel’s have the knowledge of location and amount and type of crops which they owing to their authorities can get exchanged and prevent their damaging. In a crux the objective facilitates to fill up the vacuum of despair and wastage between the stock keeper and the appropriate authority, thereby preventing this standby of all the processes which eventually leads to this futile wasting of a universal life resource in such a national that is known for its rich agricultural spectrum and errands!
